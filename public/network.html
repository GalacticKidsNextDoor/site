City-as-a-Service

Access to OPEN INTERNETs is as crucial to HUMAN LIFE and GROWTH as access to clean drinking WATER.

The Public Augmented Reality Kinectome (PARK) is an open internet providing the essential services of a city to a given area. It is a mobile municipality.

The Little Free Digital Library (LIBRARY) is a PARK application that lets residents browse little free library collections near them. Decentralized networks provide a host of realtime library services including book delivery, publication, promotion, and sales.

Anyone can set up their own PARK to create apps that leverage decentralized ledgers, robotics, and the resurgence of in-person culture to enrich our lives and communities.


Key Features

  • Delivery of groceries, medication, books, and other items
  • Security from external threats to the community (e.g. health, firearms, assault)
  • 21st Century Third spaces (virtual, in-person, mixed), to foster community
  • Programmable assets that can accomplish custom tasks ranging from garbage collection to exercise and entertainment
