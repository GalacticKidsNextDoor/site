City-as-a-Service

Access to open internets is as crucial to human life and growth as access to clean drinking water.

The Public Augmented Reality Kinectome (PARK) is an open internet providing the essential services of a city to a given area. It is a mobile municipality.

The Little Free Digital Library (LIBRARY) is a PARK application that lets residents browse collections near them and leverage networks of robots for realtime services including book/item delivery, publication, promotion, and sales.
